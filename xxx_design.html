Design Document

Here at Lily's Flower Shop you can browse through our selection of flowers for any event or special ocasion.

Our Mission Statement: The website aims to allow new comers and loyal customers to view our information and get services towards their flower needs, along with helping our shop grow and bloom like our flowers.

Our navigation tools such as our nav bar at the top of the page, the drop down menu and the external links at the bottom of the page are the the way you can get around this website. The organization structure will be layed out from the top horizontally and at the bottom our external links are layed out along with the content in the middle of the body tags.

The way i decided to put the website together was initially to start it off like chade's bicycle company was layed out only have a little more creative freedom with this site such as re-organizing the navbar, the title and the logo for the site.
